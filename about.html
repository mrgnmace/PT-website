My Story

My name is Bohdan! I’m a young and highly driven fitness coach here at Cambridge PureGym Leisure Park. My passion for fitness began at an early age, and I’ve been dedicated to improving myself both physically and mentally ever since. Over the years, I’ve made significant progress, and when it came time to choose a career path, I decided to dive deeper into fitness, expand my knowledge, and ultimately qualify as a Personal Trainer.

Originally from Ukraine, I moved to the UK in 2022 to grow as an individual and achieve my personal and professional goals.

My fitness journey started in a small local gym in Ukraine when I weighed just 56 kg. Back then, I was often called the "small guy," but I knew I could change that narrative. After two years of intense and consistent training, I gained an impressive 48 kg of primarily muscle mass. Since then, I’ve never been called skinny again.

However, life has its challenges. Following a series of stressful events, I allowed myself to gain excessive body fat. Determined to prove my knowledge and professionalism, I embarked on a personal fat-loss journey, which I’ve documented on my Instagram page. (Spoiler: I’ve already lost over 10 kg!)

Now, I’m here to help you achieve your fitness goals and improve your life. Whether you’re looking to lose fat, gain muscle, or simply feel better in your body, I’m ready to guide you every step of the way.
